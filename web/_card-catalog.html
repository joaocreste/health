AI card · the standardization target

This is the #1 thing to unify. The same concept — an amber AI interpretive card — exists in 5 incompatible forms across Joao's pages and the dynamic renderer. The standard should be ONE component with variants (synthesis / commentary / per-result / inline). Below are the forms that share styles.css.

Two more forms exist in JS and aren't shown here: .ai-ins-block / .ai-card / .ai-sevedge (the dynamic dashboard decorator) and .lab-ai-cf (per-result amber explanation). Both self-inject their CSS — relocating + merging them into one canonical amber-card component is the open task.

Tier 2 / 3 · deferred (decide per type later)

Domain specials (InBody silhouette, AUDIT bar, Wheel of Life, range selector — page-local CSS, not in styles.css) and the bespoke narrative tail (archetype, psych-dim, sp-*, letter-*). Not part of the v1 standard; triaged individually in a later pass.
