## Provenance classification — narrative

A journal/writing is a **narrative** source. Only the **authored date** applies
(`writings.written_at`); the system ingestion timestamp is `writings.created_at`. No
exam date, no requesting/performing doctor, no lab name/city/country — those are all
`n/a`. Do not fabricate a clinician or facility for a personal text.
